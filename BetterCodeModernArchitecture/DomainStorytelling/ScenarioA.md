## Scenario A: Renting a Bike (Customer flow)

1. The customer enters email address and password into the login form.
2. The app displays a map with bike rack locations to the customer.
3. The customer selects a bike rack on the map.
4. The app shows the customer the list of available bikes at that rack, including type, saddle height, and rack number.
5. The customer selects one or more bikes from the availability list.
6. The customer sends a reservation request to the app.
7. The app confirms the reservation and shows the customer the assigned rack numbers.
8. The customer walks towards the reserved bike rack.
9. The app detects proximity to the rack and activates the unlock button for the customer.
10. The customer taps the unlock button to release the bike from the rack.
11. The customer rides the bike.
12. The app shows the customer a map of racks with free return slots.
13. The customer places the bike into a free slot at a rack and locks it using the blue rack button.
